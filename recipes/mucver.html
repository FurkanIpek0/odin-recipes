Mücver Recipe

Description:

Mücver is a popular Turkish dish made from grated vegetables, typically zucchini, mixed with eggs and flour to create savory fritters. The mixture is seasoned with herbs and spices, then pan-fried until crispy and golden. Mücver can be served as a side dish or as a light main course and is often enjoyed with yogurt or a fresh salad.

Ingredients:

  • Zucchini
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Parsley
  • Dill
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Olive oil

Steps:

  1. Grate the zucchini
  2. Drain excess moisture
  3. Mix with eggs and flour
  4. Add herbs and seasoning
  5. Fry in olive oil until golden
  6. Drain on paper towels
  7. Serve with yogurt
