Kısır Recipe

Description:

Kısır is a traditional Turkish dish made from bulgur wheat. It is commonly used as a cold salad or side dish. Kısır is prepared by mixing fine bulgur with a tangy tomato paste, a variety of herbs, and spices. It often includes ingredients like cucumbers, tomatoes, and lettuce, making it a refreshing and flavorful dish. It is typically served as part of a meze spread or as a light meal.

Ingredients:

  • Bulgur
  • Tomato paste
  • Lemon juice
  • Olive oil
  • Parsley
  • Mint
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps:

  1. Soak the bulgur
  2. Mix with tomato paste
  3. Add lemon juice and olive oil
  4. Incorporate herbs and vegetables
  5. Season with salt and pepper
  6. Chill before serving
